# Market Expansion Analysis Using SQL - MondayCoffee

## Business Intelligence Project

The goal of this project is to analyze the sales data of Monday Coffee, a company that has been selling its products online since January 2023, and to recommend the top three major cities in India for opening new coffee shop locations based on consumer demand and sales performance.

## Project Contents

This repository contains SQL analytics for a coffee business, providing data-driven insights to guide strategic decision-making across three key areas:

### 1. Market Potential Analysis
- Estimated coffee consumer base by city
- Market penetration metrics
- Population-to-customer conversion rates

### 2. Sales Performance Optimization
- Product performance by region
- Seasonal sales trends
- Customer spending patterns

### 3. Operational Efficiency Insights
- Revenue-to-cost ratios
- Location profitability analysis
- Growth opportunity identification

## Technical Implementation
- SQL queries for data extraction and transformation
- PostgreSQL-compatible syntax

## Key Business Questions Answered
1. Where are our highest potential markets?
2. Which products perform best in which regions?
3. How do seasonal trends affect our sales?
4. What is our cost-to-revenue ratio by location?
5. Where should we focus our expansion efforts?

## Top Expansion Recommendations

After analyzing the data, the recommended top three cities for new store openings are:

### 1. Pune
- Very low average rent per customer
- Highest total revenue
- High average sales per customer

### 2. Delhi
- Highest estimated coffee consumers (7.7 million)
- Highest total number of customers (68)
- Average rent per customer is reasonable (330)

### 3. Jaipur
- Highest number of customers (69)
- Very low average rent per customer (156)
- Good average sales per customer (11.6k)
